MONIKA AGARWAL

Monika Agarwal, holds a master's degree in Orthodontics, a branch of Cosmetic Dentistry where artistic skills are involved. From her early life, she always had an inclination towards Art and Creativity. Being in Jaipur, got interested in Jewellery Designing and completed a Jewellery Designing course from a renowned institute in Jaipur. Due to her keen interest, started designing jewellery for family and close friends. For the last few years she has been pursuing and creating exclusive, one of its kind, customized jewellery.

Fine Jewellery has taken birth from an ideology driven by the fascination of the creator towards Rajasthani Art and Architecture which is the inspiration for many of her designs. The Designs are a fine amalgamation of the old world charm and the new trendy sparkle. Every piece of jewellery is created by putting the heart and soul and taking in consideration what the client fancies. We make “Heirloom Jewellery” that can be passed onto generations. We also recreate old jewellery pieces and design an altogether new piece from your old jewellery without damaging the old piece. We use hand-selected precious and semi precious gemstones and pearls with natural hues, outstanding sparkle and vivid shapes.
